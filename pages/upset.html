Our feelings- especially sadness-can be very tricky to understand, and even more difficult to control. When something upsets us, the feeling of sadness often manifests both emotionally and physically.
Sadness can often seem to be engulging us, and it feels like there are no ways to let our emotions out expect by crying. However, tearing up in a moment of sadness often ends up bringing feelings of guilt and shame, instead of providing us a sense of relief. This happens because many of us are taught that crying is a sign of weakness.
